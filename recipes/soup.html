Ingredients

Nuggets

  • 1 1/2 pounds skinless, boneless chicken breasts and/or thighs
  • 2 tablespoons fine dry breadcrumbs
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1/3 cup cornstarch
  • 1/3 cup self-rising flour
  • 1/3 cup sliced green onions
  • 3 cups vegetable oil for frying, or as needed

Sauce

3/4 cup mayonnaise 2 tablespoons honey, or to taste 3 tablespoons Sriracha hot sauce, plus more for serving 1 tablespoon soy sauce 1 teaspoon sesame oil 3 tablespoons rice vinegar 1/2 teaspoon ground Sichuan pepper (optional)
